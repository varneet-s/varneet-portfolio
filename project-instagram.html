Problem & approach

The brand had no Instagram presence and relied mostly on word‑of‑mouth. The challenge was to quickly build awareness and a repeatable funnel from content to ticket purchase.

  • Analysed 8–10 competitor accounts to benchmark posting frequency, content types, and engagement.
  • Designed a 3‑pillar content strategy: educational, engagement, and promotional posts.
  • Created a weekly content calendar and a tracking sheet in Excel / Google Sheets.
  • Defined KPIs: followers, reach, saves, link clicks, and ticket conversions.
  • Ran small experiments on posting times, formats (Reels vs carousels), and calls‑to‑action.
Analysis & insights
  • Used Instagram Insights to track per‑post performance and identify high‑performing formats.
  • Built simple Excel dashboards summarising weekly growth and engagement trends.
  • Identified which content types most often led to profile visits and link clicks.
  • Compared performance during campaign weeks vs baseline weeks.
